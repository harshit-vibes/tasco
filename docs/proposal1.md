[TA1] Comprehensive Customer Lifecycle Management & Multi-channel Experience Personalization based on 360-degree Profile

Project Details
Problem Statement
Business Information
Tasco Auto (https://tascoauto.com) is a leading unit in the field of automotive distribution in Vietnam, owning a network of more than 100 showrooms nationwide and a distribution portfolio of 16 car brands, including key brands such as Toyota, Ford, and Volvo. With a solid financial foundation, a comprehensive automotive service ecosystem, and an experienced management team, Tasco Auto aims to become the number one choice for Vietnamese people in automotive services, while gradually expanding its position in the region.

Problem Statement
Online leads are not fully optimized; lead distribution is suboptimal; sales follow-up is not timely, relying on individual experience. Customer experience is inconsistent, feedback data is fragmented (call, chat, email...). Marketing activities are dispersed, promotions are not personalized, and campaign effectiveness is low. Customer data is fragmented, inaccurate, and lacks a unified Customer 360 Profile.

Relevant AI Technologies
Other: Machine Learning (Classification, Ranking), NLP (Speech-to-Text, Sentiment Analysis), AI Recommendation (Recommendation Systems), Generative AI, and Customer Data Platform (CDP) & Data Standardization.

Expected Outcomes / Success Metrics
Increase lead-to-contract conversion rate, efficient lead distribution.
Standardize CS quality, early detection of experience bottlenecks.
Personalize messages/promotions, increase digital marketing effectiveness.
Build a unified Customer 360 Profile, improve data accuracy, serving as a foundation for AI.
